ABOUT US

Where Flavor and Tradition Meet

Our Story


Pasinda was born out of a shared love for Indian food and a desire to make it accessible to everyone, anytime, anywhere. Our journey began with a simple idea to create a platform where people can experience the essence of Indian flavors leaving the comfort of their homes.


Our Mission


Our mission at Pasinda is to redefine the online food delivery experience by offering not just delicious meals but also a culinary adventure that tantalizes your taste buds and transports you to the vibrant streets of India. We strive to provide exceptional service, quality ingredients, and authentic recipes that capture the essence of Indian cooking.


Meet Fenyx: Your Culinary Guide


Meet Fenyx, our trusty chatbot companion who is here to assist you every step of the way. Whether you're craving a classic Pav Bhaji or want to explore the flavors of regional specialties, Fenyx is here to help you navigate our menu, place orders, and even track your delivery in real-time.


Set Sail for Flavorful Adventure


At Pasinda, we invite you to embark on a flavorful journey with us. From the fragrant spices of North India to the fiery curries of the South, each dish tells a story of tradition, culture, and passion. So, join us as we set sail for a culinary adventure like no other.


Thank you for choosing Pasinda. We look forward to serving you a delicious taste of India!
